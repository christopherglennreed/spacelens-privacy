Slice Pane — Privacy Policy

Effective: February 1, 2025

Overview

Slice Pane is a window management utility for macOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data Slice Pane collects — which is none.

Data Collection

Slice Pane does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your Mac and does not communicate with any external servers.

Accessibility Permission

Slice Pane requests macOS Accessibility permission solely to move and resize windows on your screen. This permission is required for the app to function. No information about your windows, applications, or usage is recorded or transmitted.

Analytics

Slice Pane does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.

Third-Party Services

Slice Pane does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect data.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to: support@slicepane.com
