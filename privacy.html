Privacy Policy for SpaceLens

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Overview

SpaceLens is a disk space visualization utility for macOS. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Simple Summary: SpaceLens does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

SpaceLens does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or information. The app operates entirely locally on your device.

What SpaceLens Does

  • Scans folders and files on your Mac to calculate disk usage
  • Displays file and folder information in a visual treemap
  • All scanning and processing happens locally on your device

What SpaceLens Does NOT Do

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your usage
  • Does not send any data to external servers
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not access the internet
  • Does not share any information with third parties

File Access

SpaceLens only accesses files and folders that you explicitly grant permission to scan through macOS's standard file access dialogs. The app is sandboxed and follows Apple's security guidelines.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
