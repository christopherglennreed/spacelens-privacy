Slice Pane — Support

Getting Started

Slice Pane lives in your menu bar. After launching, click the icon in the top-right of your screen to see available snap zones.

On first launch, macOS will ask you to grant Accessibility permission. Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility and enable Slice Pane.

Keyboard Shortcuts

All shortcuts use Ctrl+Option as the modifier. Press the same key repeatedly to cycle through sizes.

  • Ctrl+Option+Arrow keys — Halves, thirds, fullscreen, center
  • Ctrl+Option+U/I/J/K — Quarters and sixths (enable in Preferences)

Cheat Sheet

Hold Ctrl+Option for half a second without pressing another key. A visual overlay will appear showing all available snap zones.
